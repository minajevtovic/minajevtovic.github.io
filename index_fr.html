Main

Effects of orthographic consistency on speech sound processing

The aim of this project is to investigate the effects of orthographic consistency on both perception and production of speech sounds.

Orthographic Expectations in novel word learning

The aim of the project is to explore whether skilled readers generated preliminarly orthographic representations solenly from aural exposure to novel words.
